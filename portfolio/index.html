Portfolio

  • Scoren met Sporen
    My graduation project for ProRail. This game was created by a team of 12 people (10 programmers, 2 artists). It was made with Monogame as its framework, using C#. We used the Scrum approach to have every memeber of the team be able to contribute properly. This game translates a boardgame to a digital format. It is used internally by ProRail to teach new employees about the responsibilities of the company. My biggest responsibility during the project was the in-game level editor.
